Ultimate Survivor Series

Create the ultimate 5-man Survivor Series team by drafting wrestlers from across WWF/WWE history.

Fill all five positions: Main Eventer, Powerhouse, Specialist, Entertainer and Team Captain.

Use your one re-spin wisely and aim for a perfect 5-0 record.

🏆 Top 10 teams earn a place in the Hall of Fame.

How to Play

You need to select 5 wrestlers to create your Ultimate Survivor Series Team.

  • Your team must contain 1 Main Eventer, 1 Powerhouse, 1 Specialist, 1 Entertainer and 1 Team Captain.
  • The Team Captain can be any category.
  • Once a year has been used, it cannot be used again.
  • Once a wrestler has been selected, they cannot be selected again.
  • You are allowed one re-spin for the entire game. Use it wisely.

Wrestler ratings represent their importance and presentation during that specific calendar year. Ratings are not intended to compare wrestlers across different eras.
